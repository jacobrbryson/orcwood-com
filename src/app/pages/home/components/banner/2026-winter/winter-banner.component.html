Winter Introspection Art Challenge

How to participate

December 21 – March 21 (Solstice to Equinox)

Welcome to Winter Introspection—a gentle, community-driven art challenge designed for reflection, creativity, and play.

Your challenge: Create three self-portraits, each in a different medium, between December 21st and March 21st.

Guidelines:

  • Three unique media: You choose what counts as a different medium. Pencil vs. charcoal vs. Bic pen? Fine. Watercolor, digital, collage, clay—also fine.
  • No photographs as one of the three primary media. (You can photograph your artwork for submission.)
  • This is not a judged competition. Submissions are shared only for community, encouragement, and accountability.
  • No policing. Everyone defines their own level of challenge, adds their own constraints if desired, and supports others without critique unless explicitly invited.
  • Early submissions are welcomed. If someone finishes all three on January 1st—fantastic.

Submission Milestones:

  • Self-Portrait #1: Due January 21
  • Self-Portrait #2: Due February 22
  • Self-Portrait #3: Due March 21 (Equinox)

The goal is simple: to make art, explore ourselves, and notice how the light slowly returns as winter unfolds. Share progress, ideas, struggles, and wins freely—this is a space for connection and introspection.
